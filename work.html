Chest Exercises

1. Push-ups: Start in a plank position, lower your body until your chest almost touches the ground, then push back up.

Back Exercises

1. Pull-ups: Hang from a bar, then pull your body up until your chin reaches the bar.

Leg Exercises

1. Squats: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the ground, then stand back up.

Arm Exercises

1. Bicep Curls: Hold dumbbells with palms facing forward, curl the weights towards your shoulders, then lower them back down.

Abdominal Exercises

1. Crunches: Lie on your back, bend your knees, and lift your shoulders off the ground, then lower them back down.

Full Body Exercises

1. Burpees: Start in a standing position, squat down, kick your feet back into a push-up position, do a push-up, then jump back to the squat position and jump up.
