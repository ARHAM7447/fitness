Push-Ups

Push-ups are a basic exercise used in civilian athletic training or physical education and commonly in military physical training.

Squats

Squats are a compound, full-body exercise that trains primarily the muscles of the thighs, hips and buttocks, quadriceps femoris muscle, hamstrings, and strengthening the bones, ligaments, and insertion of the tendons throughout the lower body.

Plank

The plank exercise is an isometric core strength exercise that involves maintaining a position similar to a push-up for the maximum possible time.

Lunges

A lunge can refer to any position of the human body where one leg is positioned forward with the knee bent and foot flat on the ground while the other leg is positioned behind.

Glute Bridges

The glute bridge is a basic exercise that targets the glute muscles, as well as the core. It is a versatile exercise that can be modified to include additional resistance.
